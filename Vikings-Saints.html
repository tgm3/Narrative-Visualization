Wild Card Round

Minnesota Vikings

vs

New Orleans Saints

Vikings Win: 26-20



New Orleans trailed by 10 at the start of the fourth quarter but tied the game with Drew Brees'
20-yard touchdown pass to Taysom Hill and Wil Lutz's 49-yard field goal. However, Cousins would hit
Rudolph with a 4-yard fade on third-and-goal, and the Minnesota Vikings pulled out a 26-20 victory
over the favored Saints in the wild-card round of the NFC playoffs on Sunday.

Minnesota's defense forced Brees into two turnovers -- one game after the Saints had finished the regular
season with an NFL record-low eight. Brees also was sacked three times and finished 26 of 33 for 208 yards,
one TD and an interception.

Dalvin Cook gained 130 yards from scrimmage and scored two touchdowns after missing the last two
regular-season games with a shoulder injury. Cousins finished with 242 yards and one TD passing.
